Our Story

Our cafe started from a concern of our family. Our humans always had and left us alone and abonded. For what felt like forever. They wanted to bring us, we could tell. But still, they always said that we were not allowed. All of us in the family thought it was very silly. So we decided to change that. And make sure the humans stay with us. Turns out they abandoned us for coffe and yummies in a beautiful enviroment. So we looked around and showed the silly humans how beautiful it is right here. At home. The humans realized that all they needed to do was put on some coffe and make some yummies. After doing that, they looked at us in our beautiful eyes and realized, that was not all they had to do. So, they started doing things that humans do. Come!
